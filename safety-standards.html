Safety First, Always

At Electro Team, safety isn't just a policy—it's our culture. Every project follows strict safety protocols.

Our Safety Philosophy

We believe that no project is complete unless it's done safely. Our commitment to safety extends beyond compliance—it's integrated into every aspect of our work, from planning to execution.

Compliance Standards

All work complies with Algerian electrical regulations, NFC standards, and international best practices.

Certified Professionals

Our team consists of licensed electricians with continuous safety training and certification updates.

Personal Protective Equipment

Mandatory use of PPE including insulated gloves, safety glasses, helmets, and arc-flash protection.

Equipment Standards

Regular inspection and maintenance of all tools and equipment to ensure safe operation.

Key Safety Protocols

1. Lockout/Tagout Procedures

  • Mandatory energy isolation before any electrical work
  • Multiple verification steps to ensure zero energy state
  • Clear tagging system to prevent accidental re-energization
  • Supervisor verification for high-voltage systems

2. Site Safety Assessments

  • Daily safety briefings before work begins
  • Hazard identification and risk assessment for each site
  • Emergency response planning specific to each location
  • Regular safety audits and inspections

3. Electrical Safety Measures

  • Voltage testing before touching any conductors
  • Proper grounding and bonding procedures
  • Arc flash boundary establishment and protection
  • Correct cable management and support

4. Emergency Procedures

  • First aid and CPR certified personnel on every site
  • Emergency contact information clearly displayed
  • Fire extinguishers and emergency equipment readily available
  • Regular emergency drills and training

Our Certifications & Compliance

Electro Team maintains compliance with:

  • NFC 15-100: Algerian standard for low voltage electrical installations
  • ISO 45001: Occupational health and safety management
  • Local Regulations: Algerian electrical safety regulations
  • Manufacturer Standards: Siemens, Schneider, and other equipment standards

Client Safety Responsibilities

For a safe working environment, we request clients to:

  • Provide clear and safe access to work areas
  • Inform us of any site-specific hazards
  • Ensure utilities are properly marked and accessible
  • Keep unauthorized personnel away from work areas
  • Follow our safety instructions during work

Continuous Improvement

Our safety program includes:

  • Monthly safety training sessions
  • Incident reporting and analysis system
  • Regular equipment safety inspections
  • Client feedback integration
  • Industry best practice adoption
